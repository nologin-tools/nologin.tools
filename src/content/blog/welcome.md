## Why nologin.tools?

The internet is full of useful tools, but most of them require you to create an account before you can even try them out. We believe that's backwards.

**nologin.tools** is a curated directory of tools that let you get things done without creating an account or logging in. Every tool listed here has been verified to offer meaningful functionality without requiring authentication.

## What makes a tool "NoLogin Verified"?

To earn the NoLogin Verified badge, a tool must:

- **Work without signup** — Core functionality is available without creating an account
- **Respect privacy** — No mandatory data collection beyond what's necessary
- **Be actively maintained** — The tool should be online and functional

We regularly health-check every listed tool to ensure they remain available and working.

## How to get involved

- **Submit a tool** — Know a great tool that works without login? [Submit it](/submit) for review
- **Add the badge** — If your tool is verified, [get the embed code](/badge) to display your NoLogin Verified badge
- **Spread the word** — Share nologin.tools with others who value privacy and simplicity
